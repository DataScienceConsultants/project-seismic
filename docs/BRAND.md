# EQ Tracker Brand

## Brand Promise

EQ Tracker helps people understand earthquakes without overwhelming them.

## Brand Voice

Calm. Human. Trustworthy. Clear.

## Notification Example

> You may have felt this.
>
> A magnitude 4.7 earthquake occurred 21 km from your location.
>
> No tsunami warning has been issued.

## Avoid

- Fear-based language
- Sensational warnings
- Speculation
- Unverified predictions
- Technical jargon on primary screens

## Product Motto

Know what matters. Stay informed. Stay calm.
