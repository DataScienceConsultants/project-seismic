# EQ Tracker Product Requirements Document

## Vision

EQ Tracker is the most trusted and beautifully designed earthquake awareness app, transforming complex seismic information into calm, personalized guidance that helps people understand what matters to them.

## Mission

Transform complex seismic data into calm, trustworthy awareness.

## North Star

> We do not show earthquakes. We explain what they mean to you.

## Target Users

Primary: everyday people who felt shaking and want to quickly understand what happened.

Secondary: earthquake enthusiasts who check seismic activity regularly.

## Core User Question

> Should I be concerned?

## MVP Features

- Live USGS earthquake data
- Mobile-first home screen
- Personal status summary
- Earthquake map
- Recent earthquake activity feed
- Geolocation support
- Magnitude and time filters
- PWA-ready foundation

## Premium Feature Direction

- Smart push notifications
- Location-based tsunami warning awareness
- Favorite locations
- Custom alert radius
- Travel mode
- Daily seismic summary
- Widgets
- Offline emergency checklist
